Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy page. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our food ordering services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing address.
  • Order Information: Details of your orders and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders.
  • To improve our services and enhance user experience.
  • To send promotional offers and updates (with your consent).

3. Data Protection

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement various security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted service providers to assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@foodordering.com.
