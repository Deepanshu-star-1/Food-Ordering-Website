Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our menu, select the items you want, and click on the "Order Now" button. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify your order within 10 minutes of placing it. Please contact our support team for assistance.

How to Use Our Website

1. Browse our menu to see available items.

2. Add items to your cart.

3. Click on the cart icon to review your order.

4. Proceed to checkout and enter your delivery information.

5. Choose your payment method and place your order.
