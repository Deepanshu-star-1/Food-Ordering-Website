Respectful Communication

Please be polite and respectful in all communications, whether you’re interacting with our staff, delivery personnel, or other users. Abusive language, discrimination, and harassment are not tolerated.

Accurate Information

Provide accurate details in your orders and profile information to ensure smooth delivery and to avoid any delays. Avoid placing orders with false information or fraudulent intent.

Timely Payment

Complete your payment promptly upon placing an order. Non-payment or attempting to circumvent payment will result in account restrictions.

Respect for Delivery Personnel

Our delivery partners work hard to bring your orders to you. Please be patient, respectful, and cooperate with them during deliveries. Any form of mistreatment will not be tolerated.

Feedback and Complaints

We value your feedback. If you encounter any issues, please report them through the proper channels. We strive to improve and provide the best service possible.

Account Security

Keep your account credentials secure. Do not share your account with others or use someone else’s account without permission. Unauthorized access will lead to account deactivation.
