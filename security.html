Security Policy

Your security is our top priority. This Security Policy outlines the measures we take to ensure the protection of your personal information when you use our food ordering services.

1. Data Encryption

We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your personal and payment information during transmission. This ensures that your data is secure and cannot be intercepted by unauthorized parties.

2. Secure Payment Processing

All payment transactions are processed through secure payment gateways that comply with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) to ensure your financial information is handled safely.

3. Access Controls

We implement strict access controls to limit access to your personal data to authorized personnel only. This includes regular audits and monitoring of access logs.

4. Regular Security Audits

Our systems undergo regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address any potential security risks.

5. User Responsibilities

While we take significant measures to protect your information, you also play a role in maintaining security. Please use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

6. Incident Response Plan

In the unlikely event of a security breach, we have a comprehensive incident response plan in place to quickly address and mitigate any potential risks to your data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our security practices, please contact us at support@foodordering.com.
